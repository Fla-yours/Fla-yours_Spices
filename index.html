About Fla(yours) Spices

We source spices from the pristine farms of Idduki in Kerala, which is also called the 'Spice Garden of India'. Every spice is earnestly handpicked to be hygienically packed and sent to your vivacious kitchens. And that explains the rustic unpolished look and purest fragrances of our spices. Adding flavours from our farms to your meals since 2018. We already enjoy a wide base of repeat customers within Mumbai itself. Come, join the club!

Pepper

Pepper or black pepper is the dried unripe fruit grown in the plant called piper nigrum.

Clove

Cloves are the flower buds of the clove tree, an evergreen also known as Syzygium aromaticum.

Cardamom

Cardamom comes from the seeds of several different plants that belong to the same family as ginger.

Cinnamon

Cinnamon is a spice obtained from the inner bark of several tree species from the genus Cinnamomum.

Nutmeg

Nutmeg is a popular spice made from the seeds of Myristica fragrans, a evergreen tree native to Indonesia.
